# Design System: Warp Future Emerald (V3 - Final)

## 1. Creative North Star (Concept)
- **Primary Vibe:** Futuristic, Cybernetic, Professional CLI.
- **Visual Metaphor:** A high-end terminal emulator window (Warp-like).
- **Core Aesthetic:** Translucent dark surfaces layered over a subtle geometric grid.

## 2. Colors (Tokens)
- **Background:** `#061a0c` (Deep Emerald Dark)
  - *Instruction:* Use with 80-90% opacity where translucency is possible.
- **Surface/Card:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.03)` (Ultra-subtle white overlay)
- **Primary Text:** `#ffffff` (Pure White, high contrast)
- **Accent/Action:** `#00ff41` (Neon Terminal Green)
- **Border/Grid:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)` (Thin white lines)

## 3. Typography
- **Font Family:** Strictly Monospaced (e.g., 'JetBrains Mono', 'Fira Code', 'Courier New').
- **Headings:** All-caps, slightly letter-spaced for a robotic/systemic feel.
- **Body:** Clean, crisp white text. No serifs.

## 4. Header & Identity (Author Signature)
- **Structure:** [Icon] - Ichihara Shu - 市原 崇 [Timestamp]
- **Target Icon (Strict Refinement):**
  - Concentric circles matching the specific balance of the provided identity image.
  - Layer 1 (Outer): Bold White ring.
  - Layer 2: Deep Blue thick ring.
  - Layer 3: Thin White ring.
  - Layer 4 (Center): Solid Red circle.
- **Placement:** Positioned at the start of every system document as a unified identity block.

## 5. Grid & Geometry
- **Background Grid:**
  - Pattern: Infinite grid of thin white lines (`0.5pt`).
  - Spacing: 15px x 15px (High density).
- **Corners:** Subtle rounding (4px - 8px).
- **Borders:** Extremely thin (`0.5px`) white or neon green borders.

## 6. Components & Elements
- **Status Indicators:** Use `[ ]` or `■` symbols. Success: Neon Green.
- **Inputs:** Prefixed with `>` or `$`.

## 7. Do's and Don'ts
- **Do:** Keep the icon, names, and timestamp on a single line.
- **Do:** Use high-contrast white text for clarity.
- **Don't:** Change the color order of the target icon (White-Blue-White-Red).
